The **Listening Lab Annotator** is an open-source platform designed for the audio annotation of less vocal and typically more challenging wildlife species. 

Traditional bioacoustic workflows struggle with sparse features of interest. This tool streamlines the process by coupling automated pre-segmentation with active few-shot learning, allowing researchers to quickly build tailored, high-accuracy models from minimal starting labels.
